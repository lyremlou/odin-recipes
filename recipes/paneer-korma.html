Paneer Korma

Description

Mughlai Paneer Korma is a super-rich and luscious curry, where Paneer cubes are added in a gravy that has goodness of dry fruits and a creamy texture from yogurt, giving it a royal touch. Kewra water and saffron adds to the royal taste of the curry.

Ingredients

To Roast

  • 4-6 Clove
  • 1 inch Cinnamon
  • 6-8 Black Peppercorns
  • 3-4 Green cardamom
  • 2 Black cardamom
  • 10-12 Cashew Nuts
  • 1 cup Browned Onion

For the curry

  • 300 g Paneer
  • 3 tablespoon Ghee
  • 3 tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • 4 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Green Chilli Paste
  • 1 cup Yogurt (Whisked with 1 teaspoon maida)
  • 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder
  • 2 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder
  • ½ teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  • ½ teaspoon Cumin Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 10-12 strands Saffron (Soaked in 2 tablespoon water)
  • 2-3 drops Kewra Essence

Steps

To roast and grind into a paste

  1. Roast all the whole spices and cashew nuts until they turn slightly browned and fragrant.
  2. Add them in a blender along with fried onion and add ¼ cup water to make a smooth paste.

For the curry

  1. Heat oil and ghee in a pan.
  2. Add panner cubes and fry until they are browned.
  3. Remove the cubes from the pan and keep aside.
  4. Add the masala paste and ginger garlic green chili paste in the same pan and fry for 3-4 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Now add yogurt, coriander powder, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, salt and ½ cup of water and fry for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add saffron soaked in water and kewra essence and mix well.
  7. Add the fried paneer cubes and mix well.
  8. Now add some water if the curry is too thick and cook for some more time.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
